NUEVA ESTRUCTURA BASE DE DATOS
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

6 NUEVAS TABLAS:

1. products
   - id, nombre, descripcion, precio, categoria, imagen, stock

2. cart
   - id, user_id, created_at, updated_at

3. cart_items
   - id, cart_id, product_id, quantity, created_at

4. orders
   - id, user_id, order_number, total, status, 
   - payment_method, payment_status, created_at, updated_at

5. order_items
   - id, order_id, product_id, product_name, price, quantity, subtotal

6. payments
   - id, order_id, amount, method, status, transaction_id, 
   - payment_details, created_at, updated_at
